About Us

About Pawan Putra Plastic Udhyog Established in 1999 under the leadership of Mr. Jagdish Vaishnav, we are a trusted name in reprocessed PVC granules for wires and cables. With 26 years of expertise , we have built strong customer trust and contributed significantly to the growth of the PVC granules industry in Rajasthan. We specialize in high-quality reprocessed granules, sourced from solar power plant cable scrap. With MoUs signed with 8+ Solar Power Plants in Western Rajasthan, we ensure a steady supply of reliable raw material. Our unique strength lies in producing FRLS (Fire Retardant Low Smoke) and ZH (Zero Halogen) granules from reprocessed PVC — making us one of the few companies in India to achieve this. Our vision is to blend performance, safety, and sustainability into every granule we produce.
